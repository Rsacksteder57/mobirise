WATCH YOU BURN is a french metalcore band, born in 2017 in the city of Metz. The project was created around the love of extrem music and three basic notions : violence, emotion and groove, with different sounds between deathcore and djent.

After two years full of work and many line up changings, the band get it actual members : Jérôme Lomre (bass), Louis Schmidt (drum), Valentin Stein (rythm guitar / clean vocals), Yohan Dully (lead guitar) and Romain Sacksteder (lead vocals).

In order to promote their first EP "GRAVITY" (2018), the five guys made many shows in the north-eastern of France, in Luxembourg and Belgium too. They were the first part in the WOLVES SCREAM's show in L'Entrepôt (Arlon, BE) with I AM NOAH in 2017 and played their songs in the Hell'Session fest (Clisson, FR).

EP "Gravity" - 2018

This debut EP was recorded, mixed and mastered in the Shadow Dog Studio in Terville (FR). We can find the five beginning compositions, created with the different inspirations of the members. 
1- Lunatic
2- Horse Of Liberty
3- No Way Out
4- 800 Miles
5- World Of Sorrow

EP "All The Rage" - 2020

The second EP was recorded, mixed and mastered with Boundless Productions in Florange (FR). Darker and more aggressive, these five tracks were written around the rage notion and all it aspects. 
1- Taste Of Ashes
2- All The Rage
3- Frozen & Broken
4- Resilience
5- Visions
